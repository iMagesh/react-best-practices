This is a standard folder structure followed in majority of the projects but some of it can be customized based on the team preferences

    
    
```bash    
  APP
    ├── src/
        ├── utils
            ├── axios-helpers.js
            ├── date-helpers.js
            └── auth-helpers.js
        ├── components
            ├── Component1
                ├── Component1.js

                └── style.css
            ├── Component2

            ├── Layouts

                    ├── Layout1.test.js
                    └── style.css
                ├── Layout2
                    ├── Layout2.js
                    ├── Layout2.test.js
                    └── style.css
        ├── containers
            ├── __tests__
                ├── Container1.test.js
                └── Container2.test.js
            ├── Container1.js
            └── Container2.js
        ├── reducers
            ├── __tests__
                ├── reducer1.test.js
                └── reducer2.test.js
            ├── reducer1.js
            └── reducer2js
        ├── actions
            ├── __tests__

 ```
